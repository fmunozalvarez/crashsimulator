# 26ai scenario 80 seeded APEX browser-session driver validation

Validation date: 2026-06-07 UTC
Target: Oracle AI Database 26ai RAC/ASM lab, PDB CRASHDB_PDB1

Design implemented:

- Added optional driver `tools/crashsim_apex_session_driver.cjs`.
- Scenario `80` keeps its current read-only APEX/ORDS continuity evidence when
  no driver is supplied.
- When `--apex-session-driver` is supplied, scenario `80` checks that the
  driver is executable and that the driver host has Node.js, Playwright, and a
  Chromium browser runtime before execution.
- The APEX test password is supplied through `CRASHSIM_APEX_SESSION_PASSWORD`
  and is not passed to the driver command line by the framework.
